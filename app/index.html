Our Services

Herb

Herbal therapy, next to dietary therapy, is the most widely used Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) treatment modality.

Moxibustion

Moxibustion is used to treat and prevent diseases, to regulate the functions of channels and zang-fu organs.

Acupuncture

Acupuncture can improve energy levels, boost the immune system, and support the physical and emotional well being of an individual.

Cupping

Cupping is used to form a localized congestion or blood stagnation to prevent or treat certain diseases.
